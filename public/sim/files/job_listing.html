Manufacturing Process Engineer

Location: Austin, TX · Full-Time · Req ID: MFG-2317

Acme Semiconductor is seeking a motivated Manufacturing Process Engineer to join our team supporting cleanroom operations and continuous improvement initiatives.

Responsibilities

  • Monitor and optimize production line yield, cycle time, and defect rates.
  • Collaborate with equipment technicians to schedule preventative maintenance.
  • Prepare reports on variance trends and recommend corrective actions.
  • Support cross-functional teams in troubleshooting process issues.
  • Ensure compliance with EHS and quality standards at all times.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical, Electrical, or Chemical Engineering (or related field).
  • 1–3 years of semiconductor or manufacturing experience preferred.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Proficiency with MS Excel; knowledge of SPC tools a plus.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

What We Offer

  • Competitive base salary and annual bonus eligibility.
  • Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision benefits.
  • 401(k) with company match.
  • Opportunities for career development and training.
